The sum of all primes: A prime number adding algorithm

Welcome to the prime number adder machine thingy! In the world of computing, prime numbers are extremely tricky as there is no simple equation for a computer to find one, but we can make a program to do this for smaller numbers.

Type in a number and you will find out the sum of all prime numbers up to and including that number. Please note this takes quite a bit of computer power so I would use numbers less than 1,000 until further optimization is made (It will probably crash your browser window) :-).
